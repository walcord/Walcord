Privacy Policy — Walcord

Last updated: 27 Aug 2025

Walcord (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a music social platform that lets users explore music and save favorite artists, albums, concerts and genres.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address used to register and sign in.
  • App activity: favorites you save (artists, albums, concerts, genres).
  • Device/technical data: non-identifying information (e.g., device model, browser, OS version) for reliability and security.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the Walcord experience.
  • Sync your favorites across devices.
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share it with third parties except as required by law or to operate essential infrastructure (e.g., hosting/service providers) under data-processing agreements.

Retention & Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. See our Delete Account page for instructions.

Children

Walcord is intended for users aged 13+.
