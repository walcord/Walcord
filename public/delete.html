Delete Account — Walcord

This page explains how to permanently delete your Walcord account and data.

Request deletion

  1. Send an email to hello@walcord.com from the same email address used for your Walcord account.
  2. Subject: “Walcord Account Deletion Request”.
  3. We will verify ownership and permanently delete your account and associated data (email, favorites, saved items).

What gets deleted: account credentials and all user-generated data tied to your account. Retention: none after deletion, except minimal records we may keep if required by law (e.g., abuse prevention).

Need help?

Contact support: hello@walcord.com
